How It All Started

I still remember the first time we met. It wasn’t dramatic. No background music. No grand moment. Just two people standing in the same space, unaware that something had quietly started.

We were polite. A little awkward. Careful with words. Careful with expressions.

I didn’t know you would become someone I talk to before I sleep. I didn’t know you would become someone I look for in a crowded room. I didn’t know you would slowly become part of my everyday life. Back then, you were just someone I met. Now, you’re someone I can’t imagine not meeting. We didn’t know this would become us. And maybe that’s what makes it beautiful.
